Shey Phoksundo

Discover the tranquility of Shey Phuksundo Lake, Nepal's deepest, nestled in the rugged Dolpa district. Our tours let you explore this gem in the heart of Shey Phuksundo National Park, surrounded by rich biodiversity and the snow-capped Kanjiroba massif. Journey with us, experience ancient Bon-po culture, and find peaceful adventure in Nepal's untouched beauty.

Madri Himal

Embark on an unforgettable journey to Mardi Himal, a lesser-known, but equally enchanting, peak in the Annapurna region of Nepal. Our tailored tours provide a thrilling trek through rhododendron forests, rustic villages, and terraced fields, leading to breathtaking views of the Mardi Himal ridge. Experience Nepal's raw beauty and tranquility, away from crowded trails.

Connect with us today.

Rara Lake

Join us in exploring Rara Lake, the largest lake in Nepal, set within the remote wilderness of Rara National Park. Our tours offer an immersive experience of this spectacular blue gem, surrounded by lush forests and snow-capped peaks. Discover the serenity of Rara Lake, and connect with nature in its most pristine form.
